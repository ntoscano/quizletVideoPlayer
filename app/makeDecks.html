How to Make a Deck

1. Click the Membright link in the navigation bar and sign up for Membright
2. Once you sign in, navigate to the "My Decks" page or click this link
3. Click "Create a New Deck".
4. Set "Deck Name" to the title of the video.
5. "Deck Image" is optional.
6. Set "Description" to the video description on YouTube.
7. Leave "Source URL" blank for now. I'll go in and set it myself after you create the deck.
8. Click "Save" and continue to make cards.

How to Make Cards

Continuing from step 8 in "How to Make a Deck":
1. Make sure "Card Type" is set to "Question and Answer".
2. Set "Question" to either a question, or something that indicates the answer, depending on the content.
Example:

Question: Where did agriculture develop?
Answer:...

This is a pretty straight forward question-and-answer card, but with something like:

Question: Advantages/Disadvantages of Agriculture:
Answer:...

The question isn't a "question", but you still have a good idea of what the answer is supposed to be.

3. Set "Answer" to the information you want to remember.
4. Click the green "+" button
5. Set "Link 1 Label" to the time of the video where this card is first relevant.

Example:
John Green first starts talking about where agriculture developed at time 3:08, so the Link Label should be "3:08"

6. Obtain "Link 1 URL" by:
Going to the video on YouTube
Going to the time set in the "Link Label"
Clicking the share tab under the video
Clicking the "Start at" box
Copying that link.
7. Repeat steps 1 - 6 for all the cards you want to make.
8. Email me at Nicolas.Toscano@gmail.com to let me know you made a deck. Please include the name of the deck you made.
